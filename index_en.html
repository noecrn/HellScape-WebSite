Project

In our group, we agreed to make a video game. According to us, it was a logical choice because we are all interested in this. We all grew up with video games. The first memory of a video game that marked all of us is the classic 2D platform game, mainly with Mario Bros. We also agreed on the nature and the type of the game: a retro style with a pixel design that recalls the first game of this type. Of course, we wanted to add our personal touch, which is why our game will be based on cooperation. It is only a multiplayer game. Two players takes part simultaneously, each one has a unique gameplay, completely different from the other one. The protagonists have opposite personalities, so we decided to make an angel and a devil, Angie and Dimy. The environment is mythological, but we decided not to base the scenario on any existing religion. Therefore, we did not mention any faith or divinity, we only drew inspiration from them. The game is not representative; it is more symbolic. The angel tries to escape from hell. From this, we decided on the name of the game: HellScape.
